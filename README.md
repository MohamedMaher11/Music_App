🎵 GHANII - Flutter Music Player App 🎶
GHANII (غني) is a beautiful and feature-rich music player app built with Flutter, bringing songs from your local phone storage to life! Enjoy your music with stunning visuals and powerful controls. 🎧

✨ Features
🎶 Play, Pause, Play/Pause Toggle: Easily control music playback.
⏭️ Next & Previous Track: Skip to the next or previous track with a simple tap.
🔄 Repeat Mode: Keep your favorite tunes on repeat.
🔀 Shuffle Mode: Shuffle your playlist for a fresh experience.
❤️ Favorite Songs: Mark songs as favorites and access them anytime.
🔥 Recently Played: Keep track of your recently played tracks.
🔍 Search & Filter: Quickly search and filter songs to find the perfect track.
🌟 Smooth Animations: A visually stunning interface with fluid animations.
📂 Local Music: Automatically fetch songs from your local storage.
🎨 Customizable UI: Enjoy a modern and sleek design for an enhanced user experience.
